About

I’m Raquel, an artist drawn to warm palettes, flowers and soft atmospheres. This portfolio is designed as a visual sketchbook where each illustration tells a small story.

My work explores calm emotions, nostalgia, and the beauty of small details. I mostly work in digital painting, but also in collage and traditional sketches.

I’m currently developing a series around flowers and rosy tones, inspired by late-summer memories and end-of-day light.
