About

Im Sophie, a self thougth artist based in Cobble Hill BC.

I work manly with oil paints, but i enjoy experimenting various medias.

I'm english and canadian, the things i love the most in life are: Listening to good loud music, thrifting and fashion, writing music and singing, hiking in the mountains, meditation, interior design, traveling to new places, my friends, family and my 4 little dogs.
